# Land Surface Temperature (LST) Mapping

![Project overview image](../assets/images/lst.png)

## Overview

Derived Land Surface Temperature (LST) from thermal satellite imagery to analyze the spatial variation of surface temperatures within the study area. The map helps identify temperature hotspots and supports studies related to urban heat islands, climate monitoring, and environmental.

**Study Area:** Vaishali, Bihar

**Duration:** Personal Learning Project (2025)

**Role:** Solo project  

**Status:** Completed

---



## Methods & Tools

**Data Sources**

- Landsat 8 (Thermal Band)
- DEM (SRTM)

**Tools Used**

* GEE
* ArcMap

---

## Key Findings

- Generated land surface temperature maps.
- Identified thermal hotspots.
- Supported environmental assessment
